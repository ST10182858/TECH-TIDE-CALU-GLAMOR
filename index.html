About Us

More Than Just A Haircut. Learn More About Us!

Welcome to Calu Glamour exotic Salon, where luxury meets style! Nestled in the heart of Pretoria at 27 Rabarber Street, our salon offers a unique blend of exotic spa treatments, soothing massages, and top-tier hair and barber services. Whether you're looking to unwind with a relaxing massage, pamper yourself with a rejuvenating spa experience, or get a fresh haircut, we are here to provide you with an unforgettable experience.

Services

What We Provide

Haircuts

Our expert stylists offer precision cuts and trend-driven styles that bring out the best in your natural beauty. Whether you’re looking for a fresh new look or a simple trim, we’re here to make it happen.

PACKAGES

For those looking to indulge, we offer tailored packages that combine multiple services at a special rate, helping you look and feel your best from head to toe..

MONTHLY PACKAGE

Our monthly memberships are perfect for clients who seek routine maintenance with a touch of luxury. Enjoy priority booking, discounts on services, and the confidence of always looking your best.

WASH

Treat your hair to a thorough cleanse and conditioning, followed by custom braiding styles that reflect your unique personality and style.

Braiding And Wig Installation

From classic braids to custom wig installations, our stylists are skilled in techniques that enhance your natural beauty and fit your personal style. We provide thorough hair care and styling to ensure your braids and wigs look flawless

FACIAL CARE

Pamper yourself with our facial treatments that refresh and rejuvenate. Each treatment is designed to address your skin’s specific needs, leaving you with a radiant, healthy glow.
